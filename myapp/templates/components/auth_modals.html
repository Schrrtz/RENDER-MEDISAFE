Data Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, date of birth, gender, civil status)
  • Contact information (address, phone numbers, email)
  • Medical information and health records
  • Emergency contact information
  • Profile photographs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide healthcare services and medical consultations
  • To maintain accurate medical records
  • To communicate with you about appointments and medical care
  • To contact emergency contacts when necessary
  • To improve our healthcare services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and the safety of our users
  • With your explicit consent
  • In case of medical emergencies

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities
